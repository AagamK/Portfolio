Tiffinwala

Web Application for Homemade Meal Delivery Tiffinwala is a web application designed to streamline the process of ordering and delivering homemade meals. This platform connects customers with local home chefs and messes, allowing users to order freshly prepared meals on a subscription or one-time basis. The service is available in multiple cities with options for pure veg and non-veg meals.

RecipeApp

RecipeApp is a Kotlin-based Android app that lets users click or upload food images to instantly get recipe details. Using APIs, it identifies dishes and provides ingredients, cooking steps, and nutritional info. The app supports both vegetarian and non-vegetarian recipes, with customizable dietary options.
